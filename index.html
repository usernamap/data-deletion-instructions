Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: June 3, 2024

If you would like to request the deletion of your data from our services, please follow the instructions below:

Contact Us

Please contact our support team at support@example.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". In your email, please include the following information:

  • Your full name
  • Your email address associated with our service
  • A brief description of the data you wish to be deleted

Processing Your Request

Once we receive your data deletion request, our team will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been successfully deleted from our systems.

Additional Information

For more information about our data deletion policies and procedures, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
